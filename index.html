KreatorAI Studio

Flutter · FastAPI · Firebase · Supabase · OpenAI · Gemini

KreatorAI Studio is a full-scale AI content creation platform I built from the ground up — from idea to live product with a real domain, payment infrastructure, and a 15+ tool suite. It exists so creators can scale their output without scaling their stress.

Built with Flutter for cross-platform reach, FastAPI on the backend, Firebase for auth and real-time data, and Supabase for credential management and subscriptions. Every tool — from script writing to AI image generation — is connected to a tiered credit system with four subscription plans.

TripWise

Next.js · FastAPI · Supabase · OpenAI · Amadeus API

TripWise takes you from "I want to travel" to "trip fully planned" in under 2 minutes. No more switching between 8 tabs for flights, hotels, itinerary, maps, visa info, and exchange rates. One platform handles it all — intelligently.

Enter your destination and dates, and TripWise runs a full Trip Viability Report — weather, crowds, visa, currency, safety — before generating a complete day-by-day itinerary with real cost breakdowns, local food recommendations, advance booking flags, and a packing list. Group trip? Invite friends, vote on activities, split bills.

EVMate

Flutter · Firebase · OpenStreetMap · Gemini AI

A full-stack mobile application built to make EV ownership genuinely effortless. Most EV apps are glorified maps — EVMate is an intelligent companion. It knows your car, understands your range, and thinks ahead so you don't have to.

Built from scratch in Flutter with a Firebase backend, it pulls real-time charging station data via OpenStreetMap and layers Gemini AI on top for smart itinerary generation — handling everything from overnight stays to 80% charge stops timed around your lunch break.
